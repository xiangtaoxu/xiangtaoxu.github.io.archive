Detailed Description

Sub-routine and functions that flag whether cohorts are resolvable or not.

These routines and functions define two logical flags (leaf_resolvable and wood_resolvable) that will decide whether cohorts can be solved in the water, energy, and CO2 budget. These flags ensure that the cohort is consistently skipped or resolved.
