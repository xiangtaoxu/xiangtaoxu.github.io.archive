Detailed Description

This module contains the derivatives of most short-term fluxes.

Three subroutines live in this module. leaf_derivs is a wrapper; leaftw_derivs solves mostly the ground derivatives (soils and temporary surface water), and canopy_derivs_two solves derivatives at the cohort scale and the canopy air space at patch level. These routines are largely based on BRAMS LEAF-2/LEAF-3 model.

Author
Adapted from LEAF-2 by David Medvigy. Further updates to make it compatible with LEAF-3 and to conserve energy and water by Ryan Knox and Marcos Longo.
Converted to module to eliminate interfaces by Marcos Longo
