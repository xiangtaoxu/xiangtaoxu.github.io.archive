subroutine load_ed_ecosystem_params ( )

This is the main loader of ecosystem parameters.

Since some compilers do not understand the assignment in the modules when the variable is not a constant (parameter), this is the safest way to guarantee it will read something (not to mention that makes compilation much faster when you want to test the sensitivity of one number).
